The Wild Network are a collection of organisations who came together around the Project Wild Thing film, and pooled resources to help kickstart a movement to reconnect kids and nature.

We developed a brand identity, custom hand-drawn type, a series of illustrations, and a website to promote the film and get the community started. We applied the visual identity across film, web, and various printed materials.

  • Project Wild Thing website
  • We also made the Wildtime mobile app.
