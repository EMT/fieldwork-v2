Volumes is a limited edition print series. Collaborating with talented artists, illustrators, designers, coders and makers, we explore a different subject in each edition. Anything goes, as long as it fits inside an A5 folder.

A Guide to Making Things is the first (and currently only) in the series. We worked with the brilliant Hey, who interpreted our eight ideas to create illustrations, which we produced as a limited run of 100 sets of postcards, and a companion website with animated versions. The eight ideas are the guiding principals we wrote when we first started Fieldwork.

This is one of our studio projects—something we do for a combination of fun, honing our skills and team, and self-promotion (in that order).
