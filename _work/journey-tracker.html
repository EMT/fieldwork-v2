Sustrans help organisations like local authorities and universities run challenge events that encourage people to change their travel habits and move around in more active and sustainable ways. They wanted a quick, simple and mobile-friendly way for participants to get involved and regularly log journeys.

We devised a very simple mobile app, for iOS and Android devices, that allows particpants to track and log their journeys in just a few taps.
