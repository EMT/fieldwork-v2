The Wildtime mobile app is an answer to the question: How can we use technology to encourage parents and kids to get away from the screen and go outside? This is an ongoing brief for The Wild Network and Project Wild Thing (and you can get involved here).

Wild time is a very simple app for iOS and Android devices. Parents, kids and teachers use it to find fun and educational outdoor activities based on the amount of time they have. Got 20 minutes to fit in some Wildtime with the kids?
