Substance work with charity, social and government organisations to gather and make sense of their data using research and technology. We worked with them to design and build a Wordpress based website, regularly updated with news, case studies and publications, to help potential clients understand the value of Substance’ services and products.

The new website has a section dedicated to promoting and engaging new customers for Substance’s digital product, Views — an online impact reporting tool for organisations. We designed a package selection process to help potential customers find the right solution, and encourage them to get in touch to start the on-boarding process.
